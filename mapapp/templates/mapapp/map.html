About GLoRS

The Global Long-term Reservoir Sedimentation dataset (GLoRS v1.0) is the first global dataset providing 40 years (1984–2023) of annual sedimentation time series for more than 1,400 of the world's most societally critical reservoirs. Open-source data for resilient water management.

×

About RECLAIM

Reservoir Estimation of Capacity Loss using AI-based Methods (RECLAIM) is a data-driven machine learning framework. It predicts annual sedimentation rates by synthesizing static reservoir geometries with time-varying hydroclimatic observations derived from satellite products.
